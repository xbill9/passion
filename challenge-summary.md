**Source:** https://dev.to/challenges/weekend-2026-07-09

"Build Something Inspired by Passion" — projects reflecting rivalry, fandom, hobbies, or devotion. World Cup timing gives it a sports angle.

- **Starts:** July 10, 2026, 2:00 AM UTC
- **Submissions due:** July 13, 2026, 6:59 AM UTC
- **Winners announced:** July 30, 2026

5 winners total, each receiving **$200 USD + DEV++ membership + exclusive badge**:
- 1 Overall Winner
- 4 Category Winners (one per sponsor tech)

1. Best Use of **Snowflake**

## Eligibility & Participation
- Age 18+
- Teams allowed: up to 4 members (one submission per team)
- One submission per person maximum
- Projects must be created *within* the challenge window
- Non-English submissions eligible for completion badges only, not prizes

## Submission Requirements
- Publish on DEV using the **`#weekendchallenge`** tag
- Include: project overview, inspiration, and demo
- Use the provided submission template
- Credit all teammates by DEV username

## Judging Criteria
- Relevance to Theme
- Creativity
- Technical Execution
- Writing Quality
- Prize Category Technology usage (optional)
- Ties broken by post reactions

## Important Rules
- No prior projects allowed (must be built during the window)
- AI usage permitted
- Open source incorporation acceptable if properly credited
